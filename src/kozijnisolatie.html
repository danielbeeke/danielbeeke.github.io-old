Kozijnisolatie

The dutch branch organization for carpentry factories (NBvT) has a lot of members that need to do a lot of difficult calculations to adhere to the law. They need to tell their customers how much warmth their wooden doors and windows let through.

This tools helps them by supplying a user interface in which they can build the whole frame digitally and set all the right parameters and get the outcome of all the formulas.

With Fonkel & Graphius, for NBvT
3 developers, lead developer
